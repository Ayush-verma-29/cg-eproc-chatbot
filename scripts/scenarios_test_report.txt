Question: What is the procedure for vendor registration on the Chhattisgarh e-Procurement Portal?
Detected Language: en
Sources Cited: CHiPS_Vendor_Registration_Manual_English.pdf, Guidelines_To_Bidders_EPS_v1.6.pdf, CRN_Customer_Reference_Number_Guide.pdf
Rule Citations: 
Elapsed Time: 53.46 seconds
Answer:
To register as a new vendor on the Chhattisgarh e-Procurement Portal, follow these steps:
1. Obtain your CRN (Customer Reference Number) by visiting CHiPS office and submitting required documents such as PAN card [Page 4].
2. After receiving the CRN certificate, use it for online registration on the portal at https://eproc.cgstate.gov.in:
 a. Click on "Supplier Registration" or "New User Registration".
 b. Enter basic details including your PAN card number and CRN [Page 4].
 c. Complete the registration form, fill in additional required information, upload the CRN certificate, and complete DSC (Digital Signature Certificate) selection process [Pages 5-6].
3. If you are already registered with a class A, B, C, or specific other classes as per government of Chhattisgarh guidelines, log in to your account on the portal using your CRN and complete any necessary updates if required .

Answer:
The Limited Tender procedure in GFR 2017 can be conducted up to a maximum of Rs. 25 lakh [Rule 162]. In this tender process, only pre-qualified or known certified vendors are notified and allowed to participate. There is no specific requirement for the number of suppliers mentioned in the context provided. However, it's important to note that details about supplier minimum requirements might be present elsewhere but they aren't explicitly stated here [Rule 162].

For Special Limited Tender Enquiry (SLTE) above Rs. 50 lakh under exceptional circumstances, there is no specific limit mentioned in the provided context regarding the number of suppliers or tender value [Not specified in GFR 2017].
